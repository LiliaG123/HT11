Meditation

Meditation helps lower cortisol levels, reducing stress and anxiety. Practicing mindfulness sharpens your ability to concentrate and stay present. Meditation can improve sleep quality by calming the mind before bedtime. It can reduce blood pressure, improve heart health, and strengthen the immune system.

Yoga

Yoga stretches and strengthens muscles, reducing stiffness and enhancing mobility. This can help with posture, balance, and overall body function. The breathing techniques in yoga (pranayama) improve oxygen flow, which energizes the body and boosts organ function. Yoga promotes mindfulness and relaxation, lowering cortisol levels (the stress hormone).

Water intake

Dehydration can cause fatigue and sluggishness, while proper hydration keeps you energized. Water helps maintain concentration, memory, and overall cognitive performance. It keeps muscles and joints lubricated, reducing the risk of cramps and improving endurance. Water helps break down food, improving nutrient absorption and preventing constipation.
